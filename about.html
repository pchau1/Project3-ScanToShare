ABOUT Scan To Share

The focus for my website is based on gathering information we see everyday into a specific and logical database that we can share to the public as well as keep private. This would be achieved by having glasses that you gather all the information that you see with your eyes. We walk around each day and see words and letters everywhere we go, but we only take in a small fraction of what we see and not all of it. We only concentrate on information we want to see which is why don’t capture all the information around us

 

HOW IT WORKS?

The information you gather will be upload to your account where you can keep public or private. In your account you will set your likes and intrests where the information you collect will sort it out in the categories you set beforehand. From there you can see the information you’ve seen. You can share it publically so the other users can see what you like and possibly contact you to meet up and make new friends. Depending on what information you have seen, you can see the individuals that live in your area that have seen similar information. As you navigate to the “results” page, it will different categories of information that has been uploaded to the site. The categories will be “featured” “popular” and “recent”, you can see what information has been gathered through out the world to see what intrests us.
